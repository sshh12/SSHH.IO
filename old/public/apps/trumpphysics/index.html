Translational Kinematic Analysis

y_0 = H_{Trump} + H_{extended} \approx 1.88m + .3m \approx 2.18m

y_f = H_{person+arms} \approx 2.3m

\Delta x \approx 3m

t_0 = 2.33s

t_f = 3.11s

g = a_{grav} \approx -9.81 \frac{m}{s^2}

\Delta t = .78s

v_{0,x} = v_0 cos \theta = \frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t} = 3.85 \frac{m}{s}

v_0 = \frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t cos \theta} = \frac{3}{.78 cos \theta} = \frac{3.8}{cos \theta}

y_f = \frac{1}{2} g \Delta t^2 + v_{0, y} \Delta t + y_0 = \frac{1}{2} g \Delta t^2 + v_0 sin \theta \Delta t + y_0

2.3 = -4.9 * .78^2 + .78 v_0 sin \theta + 2.18

2.3 = -2.98 + 2.96 tan \theta + 2.18

2.96 tan \theta = 3.1

tan \theta = 1.05

\theta_0 \approx 36^\circ

v_0 = \frac{v_{0,x}}{cos \theta} = \frac{3.85}{cos 39} = 4.76 \frac{m}{s}

v_{0,y} = v_0 cos \theta = 4.76 cos 36 = 2.80 \frac{m}{s}

v_{f, x} = v_{0, x} = 3.85 \frac{m}{s}

v_{f, y} = v_{0, y} + g \Delta t = 2.8 - 9.81 * .78 = -4.85 \frac{m}{s}

v_f = \sqrt{v_{f, x}^2 + v_{f, y}^2} = 6.19 \frac{m}{s}
